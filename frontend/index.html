Our Story

About ashmija in color

Founded in 2015 in Kallakurichi, Ashmija in Color was born from a simple belief: every wall has the power to tell a story. What began as a passion for transforming blank spaces into meaningful works of art has evolved into a creative studio dedicated to delivering exceptional mural experiences.

Our team of talented artists and designers specializes in custom wall murals, artistic installations, commercial artwork, and creative interior transformations. Every project is thoughtfully crafted to reflect the client's vision, brand identity, and the unique character of the space.

From homes and offices to hotels, cafés, schools, and corporate environments, we create murals that inspire, engage, and leave a lasting impression. Whether it's a bold abstract design, a nature-inspired masterpiece, or a custom concept, we combine creativity, craftsmanship, and attention to detail to bring every wall to life.

At Ashmija in Color, we don't just paint walls—we create experiences that connect people, elevate spaces, and tell unforgettable stories through art.

Our process begins with a detailed consultation where we understand your space, vision, and brand story. We then provide curated artist recommendations, mood boards, and digital mockups for your approval. Once the design is finalised, our team handles everything — from surface preparation and scaffolding to the final UV-resistant clear coat.

Timelines vary depending on the scale and complexity of the artwork. A small indoor accent wall typically takes 3–5 days, while a large exterior mural can take 2–4 weeks. The design and approval phase usually takes an additional 1–2 weeks. We always provide a detailed project timeline upfront so there are no surprises.

We exclusively use premium, eco-certified paints that are UV-resistant, weatherproof, and non-toxic. For outdoor murals, we apply specialised anti-graffiti and UV-protective clear coats that ensure the artwork retains its vibrancy for 10+ years. All our materials are sourced from trusted brands and tested for architectural-grade durability.

Pricing depends on factors such as wall size, complexity of the design, surface condition, location, and the artist's specialisation. We provide transparent, itemised quotes after an initial site assessment. Our projects typically range from ₹150 to ₹800 per sq. ft. — and we always work within your budget to find the best creative solution.
